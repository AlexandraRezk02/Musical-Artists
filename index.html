Chris Daughtry

Chris Daughtry is an American Rock Pop singer. He is the lead singer for the rock band Daughtry. He was also a contestant on the fifth season of American Idol.
Some of the band's most popular songs are It's Over, It's Not Over, and What About Now. But a few of my favorites are: Waiting for Superman, Outta My Head, and Feels Like Tonight.

Nathan Sharp

Nathan Sharp a.k.a NateWantsToBattle is a YouTuber. He sings covers of songs from his favorite bands, English translated anime theme songs, and video games. He also released an album filled with orginal songs called Sandcastles.
Some of Nate's most popular songs are Emperor's New Clothes, Nightmare, and Salvaged. But a few of my favorites are: Live Long Enough to Become the Hero, Victorious, and Snow Fairy (English Cover)

Linkin Park

Linkin Park is an American rock band and formed in 1996. The Band's first album was called Hybrid Theory in 2000. They also incoroprate some rap in their songs.
Some of Linkin Park's most popular songs are Numb, Heavy, and In the End. But a few of my favorites are: Valentine's Day, Breaking the Habit, and Burn It Down

Rascal Flatts

Rascal Flatts is an American country band. The three members of the band are Gary Levox, Jay DeMarcus, and Joe Don Rooney.
Some of Rascal Flatts' most popular songs are Life is a Highway, What Hurt's the Most, and Yours If You Want It. But a few of my favorites are: Come Wake Me Up, Here Comes Goodbye, and Why
